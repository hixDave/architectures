System Architecture

RAG vs Fine-tuning

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

  • Flexible integration of up-to-date knowledge
  • Easier to update and maintain
  • Clear separation of general and specific knowledge
  • Cost-effective for multiple organizations

Fine-tuning

  • Requires retraining for knowledge updates
  • Higher computational cost
  • Risk of catastrophic forgetting
  • Less flexible for multi-tenant systems
